Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

SwimSum ("we", "our", or "us") provides this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect and use information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal contact information to use the core features of the app. If you choose to sign in (e.g. with Google or email), we use that only to provide you a consistent experience and to improve the app. The app uses third-party services that may collect information as described below.

2. Third-Party Services

Advertising (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data, such as your device's Advertising ID, to provide personalized ads and perform analytics.

  • For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Analytics (Firebase)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve it. This may include anonymized data such as country, how often you use the app, workout generation behaviour, and subscription-related events (e.g. whether you have Remove Ads). We do not store your name or email address as analytics properties. Analytics data is used only for product improvement and is not shared with third parties beyond Google's analytics services.

3. Data Safety

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety policies, we disclose that:

  • App activity / usage data: Collected for analytics and product improvement (e.g. via Firebase Analytics). Not required for core app functionality. Not shared beyond Google.
  • Device or other IDs: Collected for advertising (AdMob) and analytics. Not required for core app functionality. Not shared beyond Google.
  • User IDs: If you sign in, we may associate an anonymized user identifier with analytics for product improvement only. Not shared beyond Google.

4. Children's Privacy

SwimSum is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us & Account Deletion

If you have any questions or feedback, please join the discussion in our Swim Sum Android App Test Google Group. You can also reach us via our SwimSum GitHub repository.

If you have signed in and want to request deletion of your account or data, please contact us through the Google Group or GitHub so we can process your request.
